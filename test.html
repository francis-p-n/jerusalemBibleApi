Keeping The 10 Commandments must also be kept and not overlooked. Being under Grace does not cancel out this requirement.

The True Holy Days are for everyone, and not to be monopolized by those who call themselves "Jewish" and "Israeli".

To keep these Holy Days correctly one must understand that the "New Moon" is the First day of each month, and at sunset one day ends and a new day begins. Sunrise is not the start of a new day. At sunset a new day begins. The month cycles are determined by the moon not the Calender day marked as the first day. Spring starts in the 3rd month, and Passover 14 days after the new moon of 3rd month. The new year starts in "Spring" not the middle of winter in January. According to Scripture the month of "Spring" is the first month of the year, which is the third month.


Lev:23 also mentions The first Sheaf and the Feast of Weeks, However when our Lord Yeshua/Jesus was raised from the dead not only did he become our Heavenly Lord, King and Savior, he is also our High Priest. These two Feasts have no earthy Priest to accept offerings, and Lev:23 does not mention replacing those feasts with the Day of Pentecost.


The Holy Days must be kept according to where on earth you live. If you don't live in Jerusalem keeping the Holy days based on Jerusalem time is incorrect. Be not decieved by those who instruct people to keep the Holy Days outside of what Holy Scripture instructs.
